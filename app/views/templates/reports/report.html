Key Forces Shaping the Tech Job Market

Three macro-level forces are converging to reshape hiring, skills, and career trajectories across the global tech sector:

  • 1

    AI Acceleration

    Generative AI, LLMs, and automation tools are being embedded across every software product and business process. This is increasing demand for AI-native skills while compressing demand for purely manual technical execution.

  • 2

    Remote Work Normalization

    Hybrid is now the global standard at approximately 40% of tech roles. Fully remote has stabilized around 22%, down from its 2021 peak, while on-site arrangements dropped from 78% to 38% of positions over six years â€” a permanent structural shift.

  • 3

    Global Salary Convergence

    While the US remains the highest-paying market for specialized roles, India is the fastest-growing salary market at +12–18% annual increases. Western Europe offers strong stability and benefits packages that increasingly offset headline compensation differences.
